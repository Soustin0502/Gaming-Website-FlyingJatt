Flying Jatt

Raka

Take on the role of A Flying Jatt, the reluctant superhero, in A Flying Jatt: Rise of the Guardian. Inspired by the Bollywood hit, this action-adventure game immerses you in an open-world city based on modern-day India.

Use your divine powers to protect the city from Raka, a villain born from toxic waste, and master your abilities—flight, super strength, and agility—to stop his growing threat and save the environment.
